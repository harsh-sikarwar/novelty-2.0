Our Founder

Shri Arvind Jain

Shri Arvind Jain’s journey is a testament to unwavering determination, resilience, and hard work. Starting in 1969 with a small textile shop, he dared to dream big despite the modest beginnings. Born into a humble family, he faced numerous challenges in establishing his business, but his relentless spirit and a vision for success kept him moving forward. With long hours of dedication and an eye for quality, Shri Jain gradually built a reputation for reliability and excellence in textiles. His passion for serving his customers extended beyond mere transactions; he believed in creating lasting relationships based on trust and satisfaction.

Over the years, he expanded the business to include a variety of clothing, from school uniforms to innerwear, becoming a trusted name in the industry. In the face of changing markets and economic downturns, Shri Jain’s perseverance never wavered. He constantly adapted to the evolving needs of his customers, ensuring that his products remained affordable and of the highest quality. His story isn’t just about selling fabrics; it’s about weaving dreams and opportunities for countless families. His relentless pursuit of excellence has transformed that small shop into Novelty Dresses, a brand synonymous with trust and reliability in the clothing industry.
